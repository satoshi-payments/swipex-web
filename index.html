How It Works

At Satoshi Payments, we have designed SwipEx with simplicity and security in mind. Understanding how it works is essential to fully leverage all its capabilities. Here are the key steps:

1. Registration and Setup

Your business registers on our platform as a trading agent. You verify your data and adjust preferences, including business information and employee registration.

2. Cryptocurrency Purchase

A customer interested in buying cryptocurrency approaches your business. You scan the QR code provided by the customer, containing their crypto wallet address and desired amount. Once the customer makes the payment in your local currency, you confirm receipt of payment on the platform. Cryptocurrencies are automatically sent to the customer's wallet, and the transaction amount is deducted from your balance on Satoshi Payments. You earn a commission for facilitating the transaction!

3. Cryptocurrency Sale

A customer wishing to sell cryptocurrencies approaches your business. You generate a QR code containing the crypto wallet address for reception and the amount the customer wants to sell. The customer sends the cryptocurrencies to the provided address. Once the transaction is confirmed on the blockchain, the system indicates that the payment has been received, and you can deliver the corresponding amount to the customer. Your commission and transaction amount are added to your balance on Satoshi Payments in your local currency.

4. Security and Compliance

Satoshi Payments takes care of all KYC and AML aspects, ensuring your operations are secure and compliant with regulations. Your business doesn't need to store cryptocurrencies, eliminating the risk associated with custody.

5. Account Management and Support

You access your account online or via the mobile app to monitor your transactions, balances, and commissions. Our support team is available to assist with any issues or inquiries. Satoshi Payments provides technical support and assistance to your customers.

6. SwipEx Simplifies and Streamlines

SwipEx simplifies and streamlines the process, allowing you to expand your business in the exciting world of cryptocurrencies without complications. From initial setup to daily management, we provide the complete solution for your business to thrive in this new financial era.
